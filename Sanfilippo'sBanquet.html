Sanfilippo's Banquet Room Information

Thank you for your interest in our Banquet Room services!

Here we have some useful information about our Banquet Room Service:

Our Banquet room holds a max of 50 people
We will reserve a 3 hour period for your party.
The Banquet Room has its own radio and cd player, air conditioning, and restroom.
The final food and beverage bill will be presented to the host/hostess at the end of the party and will include a 20% gratuity.
We ask the final bill meet or exceed $350.00.
The room charge is $150.00 and is in addition to the final food and beverage bill.
You may bring a cake or dessert for your party.
Sanfilippo's has its own alcohol license, so no outside alcohol will be permitted.
We will set up the Banquet Room to best accomodate your group size.
Sunday-Thursday reservations only. No Friday or Saturday reservations.
Cash or charge cards welcome.
To book the Banquet Room, please call contact Dora at (619)464-2088.
No dates are confirmed until a room charge and signed contract are received.
Thank You!
