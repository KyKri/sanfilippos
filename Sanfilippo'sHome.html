History


Sanfilippo’s Pizza was established in 1975 by Donato and Anna Sanfilippo in La Mesa, CA. Donato was of Sicilian descent and worked for an Italian restuarant before opening Sanfilippo's. We take great pride in being a family owned and operated business. In fact, there are currently three generations of the Sanfilippo family who own, manage and work the restaurant. We prepare our menu items fresh daily and have a large selection to choose from. What started as a small Italian restaurant has blossomed into a local landmark. We are known for our delicious pizzas and generous pastas. Join us for lunch or dinner. There is something for everyone! Ciao!
